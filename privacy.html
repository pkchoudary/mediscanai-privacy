Privacy Policy for MediScan AI

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

MediScan AI respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email and name when you sign up with Firebase Authentication.
  • Healthcare Reports: Uploaded blood reports are securely processed and stored in Firebase/Cloudinary.
  • Device Permissions: Camera (for scanning reports), Location (for finding nearby hospitals).
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage your account.
  • To analyze uploaded reports and provide health insights.
  • To help you discover nearby hospitals and healthcare providers.
  • To improve app performance and features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is only shared with trusted third-party services (Firebase, Google Maps, Cloudinary) to deliver app functionality.

Data Security

We use encryption and industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

Your Rights

You may delete your account or request data removal at any time by contacting us.
